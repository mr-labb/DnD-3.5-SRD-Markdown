## Power Word Stun

Stuns creature with 150 hp or less.

- **School**: Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]  
- **Level**: Sor/Wiz 8, War 8  
- **Components**: V  
- **Casting Time**: 1 standard action  
- **Range**: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)  
- **Effect**: One creature with 150 hp or less  
- **Duration**: See text  
- **Saving Throw**: None  
- **Spell Resistance**: Yes  

## Description

You utter a single word of power that instantly causes one creature of your choice to become stunned, whether the creature can hear the word or not. The duration of the stun depends on the target's current hit point total. Any creature with 151 or more hit points is unaffected by *Power Word Stun*.

### Stun Duration by Hit Points

- **50 or less** → 4d4 rounds  
- **51–100** → 2d4 rounds  
- **101–150** → 1d4 rounds
