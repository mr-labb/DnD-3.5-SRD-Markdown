## Permanency

Makes certain spells permanent.

*   Universal
*   **Level:** Sor/Wiz 5
*   **Components:** V, S, XP
*   **Casting Time:** 2 rounds
*   **Range:** See text
*   **Effect:** See text
*   **Duration:** Permanent; see text
*   **Saving Throw:** None
*   **Spell Resistance:** No
