### Point Blank Shot 
***Feat Type: General***

**Benefit:** You get a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls with ranged
weapons at ranges of up to 30 feet.

**Special:** A fighter may select Point Blank Shot as one of his fighter
bonus feats.
