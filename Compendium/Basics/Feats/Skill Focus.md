### Skill Focus 
***Feat Type: General***

Choose a skill.

**Benefit:** You get a +3 bonus on all checks involving that skill.

**Special:** You can gain this feat multiple times. Its effects do not
stack. Each time you take the feat, it applies to a new skill.
