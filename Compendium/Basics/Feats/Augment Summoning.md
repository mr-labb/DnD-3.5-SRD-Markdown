### Augment Summoning 
***Feat Type: General***

**Prerequisite:** Spell Focus (conjuration).

**Benefit:** Each creature you conjure with any *summon* spell gains a
+4 enhancement bonus to Strength and Constitution for the duration of
the spell that summoned it.
